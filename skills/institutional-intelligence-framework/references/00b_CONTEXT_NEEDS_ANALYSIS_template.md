**Status definitions:**
- `Have` — usable, reviewed context exists for this angle.
- `Archived, unreviewed` — raw material exists but hasn't been curated into Layer 3/4.
- `Unverified` — evidence exists somewhere but hasn't been checked/confirmed against this task. Do not treat as `Have`.
- `Missing entirely` — no material exists; may be a hard input gap (not fixable by re-analysis of what's already on hand).
